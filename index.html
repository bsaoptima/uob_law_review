University of Birmingham Law Review

The Law Review brings together the most passionate undergraduate and postgraduate law students and alumni of the University of Birmingham. It allows students to expand their academic endeavors by publishing both short- and long-form articles on legal principles, developments, and anything in between.

Our first issue back comes out November 1 2023, to be featured, submit your article via the submit now button!

Submission Guidelines

The University of Birmingham Student Law Review accepts articles and Case comments. Book reviews are not accepted. A short-form article eligible for publication in our monthly edition should be under 1,000 words. A long-form submission for a semestrial paper should be between 2,000 to 4,000 words. Case comments on newer cases are our preference, but should the comments demonstrate a new approach to an older case of importance,  this is also acceptable.
